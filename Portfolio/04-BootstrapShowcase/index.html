Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It is known for its strong flavor, rich aroma, and creamy texture, often topped with a layer of crema. Espresso serves as the base for many popular coffee drinks, such as cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos.

Macchiato

A macchiato is an espresso “stained” with a small amount of steamed milk or foam. The milk slightly softens the intense flavor of the espresso while maintaining its bold character. It’s the perfect balance for those who enjoy espresso with just a touch of creaminess.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. This classic Italian coffee drink is smooth, creamy, and rich, offering a harmonious blend of espresso strength and milky sweetness, often finished with a dusting of cocoa or cinnamon.
