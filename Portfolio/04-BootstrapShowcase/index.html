Frequently Asked Questions

Espresso is just a brewing method. It’s made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee under high pressure, producing a small, strong shot. Regular coffee (like drip or pour-over) uses gravity instead, making a larger and lighter cup. The beans are often the same — only the grind and method change.

Start with medium roast. They have a great flavor that’s easy to enjoy without being a enthusiast, but its a start to really enjoy coffee. Dark roasts taste stronger and smokier, while light roasts are brighter, but those are fancy words for later, really it narrows down to do you like it or not.
