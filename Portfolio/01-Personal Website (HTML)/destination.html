Munich

Munich is a city that perfectly balances tradition with modernity. The Bavarian capital offers incredible architecture, world-class museums, and a vibrant cultural scene. From the iconic Marienplatz to the peaceful English Garden, Munich has something for everyone.

  • Province/State: Bavaria
  • Country: Germany
  • Population: 1,488,202
  • Latitude & Longitude: 48.1351°N, 11.5820°E

Mexico City

Mexico City is a vibrant metropolis rich in history and culture. As one of the largest cities in the world, it offers an incredible mix of ancient Aztec heritage, colonial architecture, and modern urban life. The food scene is unmatched, and the warmth of its people is unforgettable.

  • Province/State: Mexico City (Federal District)
  • Country: Mexico
  • Population: 9,209,944
  • Latitude & Longitude: 19.4326°N, 99.1332°W

Los Feliz (Los Angeles)

Los Feliz is one of Los Angeles' most charming neighborhoods, known for its bohemian atmosphere and stunning views of the Hollywood Hills. This creative enclave is home to artists, musicians, and writers, offering a perfect blend of vintage charm and modern sophistication.

  • Province/State: California
  • Country: United States
  • Population: 35,000 (neighborhood)
  • Latitude & Longitude: 34.1192°N, 118.2437°W

Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a living museum where ancient history meets modern life. Every corner tells a story spanning over 2,000 years. From the Colosseum to the Vatican, from incredible pasta to breathtaking fountains, Rome is a city that captures the heart and never lets go.

  • Province/State: Lazio
  • Country: Italy
  • Population: 2,873,104
  • Latitude & Longitude: 41.9028°N, 12.4964°E

Porto

Porto is a coastal gem in northern Portugal, famous for its stunning riverside district, port wine cellars, and azulejo tiles. The city's blend of medieval charm and contemporary culture creates an atmosphere that's both romantic and inspiring. The hospitality of the Portuguese people makes every visit memorable.

  • Province/State: Norte Region
  • Country: Portugal
  • Population: 237,591
  • Latitude & Longitude: 41.1579°N, 8.6291°W
